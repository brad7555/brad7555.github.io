Saved Routes

After the route is created and saved, you can view it, along with all of the other saved routes within the database. This page has many different functions running at one time. You can like any of the routes that you see, as well as sort all of the routes by a specific tag. These little sections are just touching the surface of the work we put into this piece of software. The demo video does a very good job at explaining all of the functionality that comes along with this application.

The Crown jewel

Creating a route was the main idea behind this project. This is the very first page that we as a group designed, and it really got things started on a good note. Javascript is the main driver behind this page. The map, and both of the lists are updated independently, as are the liked establishments and the shortest route functions.
